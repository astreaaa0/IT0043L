Welcome to the JuJutsu Kaisen Fan Page

Jujutsu Kaisen is a Japanese manga series written and illustrated by Gege Akutami. It follows high school student Yuji Itadori as he joins a secret organization of Jujutsu Sorcerers to eliminate a powerful Curse named Ryomen Sukuna, of whom Yuji becomes the host.

Jujutsu Kaisen Chapter 262 left fans disappointed, here's why it was so short

Jujutsu Kaisen previously revealed a twisted narrative as Yuta took over Gojo's dead body. Fans initially thought that Gojo was alive and thriving again, but those hopes were shattered by the shocking revelation about who was really in control of him. As the plot thickened, fans eagerly awaited the release of Chapter 262 in hopes of learning more about the gruelling fight against Sukuna.

Main Characters

Yuji Itadori

Yuji is the main protagonist of the series. He joins the Tokyo Metropolitan Magic Technical College to protect others from Curses and to find a way to exorcise Sukuna from his body.

Megumi Fushiguro

Megumi is a first-year student at Tokyo Metropolitan Magic Technical College. He is a stoic and reserved character who is skilled in using his Shikigami to fight Curses.

Nobara Kugisaki

Nobara is a first-year student at Tokyo Metropolitan Magic Technical College. She is a confident and brash sorcerer who uses nails and a hammer to exorcise Curses.

Satoru Gojo

He was first introduced in Akutami's short series Tokyo Metropolitan Curse Technical School as the mentor of the cursed teenager Yuta Okkotsu at Tokyo Prefectural Jujutsu High School.
